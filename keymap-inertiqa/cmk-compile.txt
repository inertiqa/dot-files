- go to https://config.qmk.fm/#/crkbd/rev1/LAYOUT_split_3x6_3 and configure keymap, or upload your current keymap.json
- edit and save to you hearts content
- place keymap.json on: users/admin/qmk_firmware/keyboards/crkbd/keymaps/inertiqa
- compile firmware with: qmk compile -kb crkbd/rev1 -km inertiqa
- grab fresh baked .hex file found in: users/admin/qmk_firmware/
- open qmktoolbox.exe, load fresh .hex file, tik "auto-flash"
- unplug keyboard, split keyboard
- connect one side (secondary first?), press button on keyboard
- do both sides, disconnect keyboard
- rejoin keyboard, connect keyboard
- enjoy
